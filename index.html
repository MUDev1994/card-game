Memory Game

Instructions

Concentration, also known as Match Match, or Memory is a card game in which all of the cards are laid facedown on a surface and two cards are flipped face up over each turn. The object of the game is to turn over pairs of matching cards.

Scores

Each time you find one pair of two matching cards, you get two points. Each time you don't find, you lose one point. Try Your Best, Good Luck!

Hint

Whenever the game loads, the cards are laid facedown randomly, so cards may not be where they were in the last game you played!
